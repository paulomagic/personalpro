## Fontes Primárias

### Renaissance Periodization
- **Autor**: Dr. Mike Israetel, Dr. James Hoffmann
- **Conteúdo**: Volume landmarks (MEV, MAV, MRV), frequência por grupo
- **Site**: https://rpstrength.com
- **Recursos**:
  - *Scientific Principles of Hypertrophy Training*
  - *Hypertrophy Training Guide* (planilhas de volume)

### Brad Schoenfeld, PhD
- **Área**: Meta-análises de hipertrofia
- **Publicações-chave**:
  - *Dose-response relationship between weekly resistance training volume and increases in muscle mass* (2017)
  - *Effects of Resistance Training Frequency on Measures of Muscle Hypertrophy* (2016)
  - *The mechanisms of muscle hypertrophy and their application to resistance training* (2010)

### Eric Helms, PhD (3D Muscle Journey)
- **Conteúdo**: Muscle & Strength Pyramids
- **Recursos**:
  - *The Muscle and Strength Pyramid: Training* (livro)
  - *The Muscle and Strength Pyramid: Nutrition* (livro)

### NSCA (National Strength and Conditioning Association)
- **Certificação**: CSCS
- **Recurso principal**: *Essentials of Strength Training and Conditioning* (4th ed.)
- **Conteúdo**: Guidelines de volume, frequência, periodização

### Tudor Bompa, PhD
- **Área**: Periodização clássica
- **Livro**: *Periodization: Theory and Methodology of Training* (6th ed.)

### Vladimir Issurin
- **Área**: Block Periodization
- **Livro**: *Block Periodization: Breakthrough in Sports Training*

## Artigos Científicos Relevantes

### Hipertrofia
1. Schoenfeld BJ et al. (2017) - *Dose-response relationship between weekly RT volume and muscle mass*
2. Schoenfeld BJ et al. (2016) - *Effects of RT Frequency on Muscle Hypertrophy*
3. Krieger JW (2010) - *Single vs. multiple sets of resistance exercise for muscle hypertrophy*

### Frequência
1. Schoenfeld BJ et al. (2015) - *Influence of RT Frequency on Muscular Adaptations*
2. Grgic J et al. (2018) - *Effect of RT Frequency on Gains in Muscle Mass*

### Periodização
1. Williams TD et al. (2017) - *Comparison of Periodized and Non-Periodized RT on Maximal Strength*
2. Harries SK et al. (2015) - *Systematic Review of DUP vs Traditional Periodization*

### Recuperação
1. Damas F et al. (2018) - *A Review of RT-Induced Changes in Skeletal Muscle Protein Synthesis*
2. Schoenfeld BJ & Contreras B (2014) - *The Muscle Pump: Potential Mechanisms and Applications for Enhancing Hypertrophic Adaptations*
